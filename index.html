Skull Collector Game


Albert Raymond Cozzette

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are 4 skulls below. By clicking on a skull you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goers above the random number.


The value of each skull is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each skull.
